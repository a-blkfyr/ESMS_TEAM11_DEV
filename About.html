MicroCSE

What is MicroCSE?:

It is an innovation hackathon inspired by SpaceApps and Microsoft Garage, designed to encourage students to solve real problems encountered within ESI: website issues, outdated collaborative platforms, bugs in ESI Talents, etc. The participants will work on real challenges that affect students daily and propose tools that could be used by future generations.

Micro:

direct reference to Micro Innov, a symbol of innovation and technology.

CSE:

the scientific community of ESI. The name reflects both the international inspiration behind the event and its strong local identity within ESI.
